Garlic Pasta

Menu Class: Main Course

Description:
The new dessert menu.
Tiramisu: 
Tiramisu is a classic Italian dessert. Ladyfinger cookies dipped in coffee, then layered with mascarponed and dusted with cocoa powder. 

Ingredients
1. 8 ounces spiral pasta
2. 6 tablespoon olive oil
3. 2 cloves of sliced garlic
4. 8 tiger prawns, peeled and deveined
5. 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
6. 1 teaspoon chopped fresh parsley

Directions
1. Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil.
2. Add pasta and cook for 8 to 10 minutes or until al dante.
3. Drain, return to pot.
4. Heat oil in a large heavy skillet over medium heat.
5. Saute garlic for 5 minutes.
6. Toss in prawns, and cook for 5 minutes on each side.
7. Remove prawns and set aside.
8. Remove garlic slices and discard. (optional)
9. Pour oil over pasta in pot, and toss to evenly coat.
10. Sprinkle 3/4 of the Parmesan cheese onto pasta.
11. Stir until evenly distributed.
12. Transfer to serving dish.
13. Arrange prawns on top.
14. Sprinkle remaining Parmesan and parsley.


Directions
1. In a medium bowl beat together the egg yolks and 1/3 cup of sugar.
2. Using a wooden spoon stir in mascarpone cheese, beaten egg whites, cream and kirschwasser.
3. Stir until smooth and set aside.
4. Dissolve remaining 2/3 cup sugar in coffee.
5. Quickly, to avoid complete saturation, dip ends of ladyfingers in coffee mixture.
6. Place ladyfingers in a single layer in a 9 x 13 inch glass baking dish.
7. Spread a layer of cheese mixture over the ladyfingers.
8. Repeat layers, ending with cheese mixture.
9. Cover and refrigerate for 3 hours.
10. Sprinkle with cocoa just before serving.
